Our Vision

Mission

The Assembly of God Church School started with a passion for developing excellent individuals through education. It began to meet the educational need of the society; with an aim of building India by building better boys & girls. Today the students here experience a healthy blend of technology and global language 'English'.

The school is passionate and keen on leaving no stone unturned in teaching excellent curriculum and moral values. We believe that every child is precious, and hence special attention is given to each child. We understand the individual differences of a child, and therefore we make sure that each child develops at their own pace with freedom and guidance.
